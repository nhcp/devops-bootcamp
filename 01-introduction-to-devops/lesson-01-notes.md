# Module 1, Lesson 01: Introduction to DevOps

## 🎯 The Goal
To shorten the systems development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality.

## ⚠️ The Problem: The Wall of Confusion
Traditionally, "Dev" and "Ops" were separate silos. This caused bugs and crashes because developers didn't know if the servers could handle their code.

## ✅ The Solution
DevOps combines Development and IT Operations to automate infrastructure and ensure code flows smoothly from a laptop to a live server.
