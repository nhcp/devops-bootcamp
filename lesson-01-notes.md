The Conflict: Traditionally, "Development" and "Operations" were separate silos. "Dev" created software, and "Ops" was responsible for keeping it running.


The Problem: This caused "The Wall of Confusion," where developers pushed code without knowing if the server could handle it, leading to bugs and crashes.


The DevOps Solution: It is the practice of combining Development (Dev) and Information Technology Operations (Ops).


The Goal: To shorten the systems development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality.


DevOps Engineer Responsibilities: You aren't just a coder; you are the bridge that automates the infrastructure and ensures the code flows smoothly from a laptop to a live server.
